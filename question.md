# Survey Questions

**Q1:** What is your name and business name?
**Q2:** What type of business do you run? (Distributor, Wholesaler, Both, Other)
**Q3:** How many buyers/customers do you supply regularly? (1-10, 11-50, 51-200, 200+)
**Q4:** How do you currently track your inventory and stock? (Excel/Spreadsheet, Physical notebook/ledger, WhatsApp messages, Accounting software, No system, Other software)
**Q5:** How do your buyers place orders with you today? (WhatsApp message or call, Phone call only, Visits in person, Email, Online platform/website)
**Q6:** What is the single biggest problem in running your distribution business right now?
**Q7:** Which of these problems cost you money or time most often? (Stockouts, Wrong orders, Unpaid debts, Manual invoicing, No visibility, Delivery issues, Staff errors)
**Q8:** How much time per week do you spend on admin: invoices, tracking stock, chasing payments? (Less than 2 hrs, 2-5 hrs, 5-10 hrs, 10+ hrs)
**Q9:** If there was a simple app that let you track stock, receive buyer orders, send invoices, and see what's selling, how useful would that be to you? (1 to 10 scale)
**Q10:** What would make you hesitate to use a new software for your business? (Too expensive, Too complicated to learn, Poor internet/data cost, My staff won't use it, I don't trust new Nigerian tech products, My current system works fine)
**Q11:** How much would you be willing to pay monthly for software that genuinely solved your biggest problem? (Nothing, ₦5,000-₦10,000, ₦10,000-₦15,000, ₦15,000-₦30,000, ₦30,000+)
**Q12:** Is there anything else you want us to know or a question you wish we had asked?
**Optional:** Can we follow up with you for a 10-minute call? (WhatsApp number)
